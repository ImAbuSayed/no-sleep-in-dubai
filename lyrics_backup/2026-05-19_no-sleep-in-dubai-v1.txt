[Hook 2]
Mere saahan vich tu ae my only one
Tainu vekh ke main rukda haan totally stunned
Aaja nede mere kol baby take a chance
Main taan tere pichhe rulda haan in this trance

[Verse 1]
Ucche chubareyan te khada watching the skyline
Tera nakhra taan karda ae out of my mind
Kaali raat de pehre ch we own the night
Main tera hath fad-da haan holding it tight
Tu kaale libaas vich looking so fine
Tere pichhe main turda haan crossing the line
Ishq di is agg vich let the sparks fly
Main taaran nu takkda haan under this sky

[Pre-Chorus]
Raat de is saaye ch main darda nahi
Tere bina kise te main marda nahi
Aaja mere saahan vich feeling the fire
Main taan bas tera haan my only desire

[Chorus]
Dubai di is raat ch baby all night long
Tere naina de jaadu vich I belong
Main tainu labhda haan searching so far
Tu hi meri manzil ae my shining star
Ret diyan lehran te leaving our trace
Main tere naal udd-da haan into space
Tu asmaan di pari feeling so right
Main tainu chaunda haan holding you tight

[Verse 2]
Hawa nu cheer ke main tearing the wind
Tere naina vich dubbda leaving my sins
Milon di doori te leaving a mark
Main parchaawa ban turda haan in the dark
Dhool ud-di raste te burning the floor
Tu ishaara jado kardi wanting some more
Main sab kujh chhad aunda haan with all my might
Dil jitteya main tera ready to fight
